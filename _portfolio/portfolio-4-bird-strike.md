**GitHub:** [crushWu8/Bird-Strike-Analytics](https://github.com/crushWu8/Bird-Strike-Analytics)

Analyzed FAA wildlife strike data to understand safety risk, financial costs, and operational disruptions. Tracked trends in bird strikes over time at national and regional levels, identified key drivers including flight phase, aircraft type, airport, and seasonality.

Estimated impact through damage categories and proxy cost signals. Built simple predictive models to forecast near-term incidents and illustrate limitations of linear extrapolation.
