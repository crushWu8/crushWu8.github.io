We analyze graph burning and cooling processes on strong products of paths. We establish bounds on the burning and cooling numbers and derive results for the critical number k* using generating functions and combinatorial tiling arguments. The liminal burning on paths component provides a lower bound for k* that captures the transition between burning regimes.
